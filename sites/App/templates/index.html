Welcome To Medarametla Anjamma Mastan Rao PG College


Medarametla Anjamma Mastan Rao Post Graduate college located in Kesanapalli Village, Narasaraopet Mandal, Palnadu District, State of Andhra Pradesh. The college is recognized by AICTE and affiliated to Acharya Nagarjuna University. The college offers courses MBA, Master of degrees (2 year ) duration. The college has always in the path of serving the society with a vision that enlightenment can empower Students through proper education and make them fruitful individuals for the society and college known for the best standards of stringent discipline, admirable academic excellence and outstanding extra-co-curricular activities..
