About MAM

Medarametla Anjamma Mastan Rao College of Pharmacy (M.A.M) was established with a group of eminent academicians and professionals from different field with a mission to offer quality education with a moto to provide education to all and contribute itself to the millennium development goals. Since its inception, M.A.M. has been successful in providing quality education with state – of – art facilties and emerging as a leader in the field of pharmacy education.The mission is to train the enthusiastic students and transforming them as knowledgeable pharma professionals to meet the society at local, national and global level. Within short time the college has been upgraded to PG level institutions. Facilities like International student exchange programme, personality development programme, IPA, APTI programmes, NSS unit, will help the students to groom themselves to international arena. Guest lecturers by experts from industry, different universities, tie up with multi-national companies, organizing and attending various national & international seminars, visit of top MNC’s and placement cells are unique features of M.A.M.

Why You Choose Us
